Help & FAQ

How do I create a job post?
Set your display name at the top, fill in the job details, and click Save Post. You can also add an image or a link.
How do I edit or delete my post?
You can only edit or delete posts you created (matching your display name). Use the Edit or Delete buttons on your post card.
How do I add or remove announcements?
Click Add Announcement in the Announcements panel. You can edit or delete any announcement using the buttons next to it.
Why do I need a display name?
Your display name identifies you as the author of your posts. It is stored only in your browser for now.
Where is my data stored?
All posts and announcements are saved in your browser's localStorage. When backend integration is ready, data will sync with the server.
Who can see my posts?
Currently, posts are only visible on your device. In the future, posts will be visible to all users when backend sync is enabled.
