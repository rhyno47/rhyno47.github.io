Welcome to Mwanza

Mwanza, known as the “Rock City,” sits majestically on the shores of Lake Victoria. Its dramatic rocky hills, calm lake waters, and vibrant markets create a unique experience for travelers. Here, the past meets the present—fishermen still cast their nets in traditional ways while the city thrives with modern life. Explore the streets, enjoy local cuisine, and experience the warmth of Mwanza’s people.

Lake Victoria

The largest lake in Africa, perfect for boat rides, fishing, and enjoying stunning sunsets.

City Views

From the hills, the city stretches beautifully along the lake, showing a mix of nature and urban life.

Local Markets

Colorful markets filled with fresh fish, fruits, and handmade crafts showcase the city's culture.

Hotels

Mwanza offers a variety of accommodations for travelers seeking comfort and beauty. From lakeside resorts to cozy guesthouses, you can enjoy modern amenities while being close to nature. Whether you're here for business, adventure, or relaxation, Mwanza’s hotels cater to all preferences.

Mwanza Resort

Sunset Hotel

Enjoy breathtaking lake views and peaceful surroundings at this elegant hotel.

Places to Visit

Mwanza is rich with attractions for every kind of traveler. Explore iconic rock formations, peaceful beaches, and cultural sites. Nature lovers will appreciate Saanane Island National Park, while history enthusiasts can enjoy museums and local art galleries. The vibrant city life offers markets, music, and festivals that celebrate the Sukuma culture.

Saanane Island

A national park on the lake, home to monkeys, birds, and scenic walking trails.

Bismarck Rock

Iconic rock formation offering panoramic views of Mwanza and Lake Victoria.

City Markets

Vibrant markets with fresh produce, crafts, and a taste of local life.
